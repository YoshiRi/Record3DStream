# Privacy Policy for Record3D Stream

**Last Updated: March 23, 2026**

**PathOn Robotics** ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Record3D Stream mobile application (the "App").

## Data Collection and Use

Record3D Stream captures the following sensor data from your device **only during active streaming sessions**:

- **Camera (RGB):** Used to capture color images for 3D streaming.
- **LiDAR / Depth Sensor:** Used to capture depth maps for 3D streaming.
- **Motion Sensors (Accelerometer & Gyroscope):** Used to capture IMU data for pose estimation.
- **Camera Pose (ARKit):** Used to track device position and orientation.

## How Data Is Transmitted

All captured data is streamed in real-time over your **local network (WiFi) or USB connection** directly to a client device (e.g., your computer) that you connect manually. Data is transmitted only when you press "Start Streaming" and a client is connected.

## Data Storage

Record3D Stream **does not store any captured data** on the device or on any remote server. All data is streamed in real-time and discarded after transmission.

## Third-Party Services

Record3D Stream **does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services**. No data is sent to external servers.

## Data Sharing

We **do not collect, store, or share** any personal data or sensor data with any third party.

## Children's Privacy

Record3D Stream does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on our website.
